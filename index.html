About Me

Academic Objective

To contribute effectively in teaching, research, and academic development by utilizing my knowledge, skills, and experience in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. I aim to work in a dynamic academic environment that encourages learning, innovation, and professional growth while making meaningful contributions to students and the institution.

Role & Responsibilities

Currently serving as the Head of the Department for Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning. In addition to academic leadership, I coordinate projects, seminars, workshops, and student activities, including acting as the Institute Webmaster and mentor.

Research Interests

Smart Grid Analytics

Focusing on electricity theft detection, load forecasting, and anomaly detection using machine learning and deep learning architectures.

Healthcare AI

Developing Explainable AI models for medical imaging diagnostics, specifically bone fracture detection and clinical interpretations.

Precision Agriculture

Designing multi-data fusion systems and computer vision networks for crop health monitoring and plant disease identification.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Enhancing transparency in black-box deep learning models to ensure trust, safety, and accountability in high-risk applications.
